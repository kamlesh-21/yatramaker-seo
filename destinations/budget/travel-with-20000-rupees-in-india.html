Where Can I Travel With ₹20,000 in India? (2026 Realistic Guide)

Yes – ₹20,000 is enough for a memorable domestic trip in India, especially if you plan smartly. Here's what you can realistically expect and the best destinations.

Assumptions for This Budget

  • Duration: 4–5 days (including travel)
  • Travel mode: Train or bus (budget flights rare)
  • Stay: Budget hotels / homestays (₹800–1,200/night)
  • Food: Local eateries + 1 restaurant meal/day
  • Starting city: Major Tier-1 or Tier-2 (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, etc.)
  • Season: Shoulder / off-peak (cheaper)
