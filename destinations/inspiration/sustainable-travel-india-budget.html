Sustainable Travel in India on a Budget

Traveling responsibly doesn't have to be expensive. In fact, sustainable choices often save money while protecting India's beauty.

Top Sustainable Travel Tips That Also Save Money

  • Use public transport – Trains & buses are cheap & low-carbon
  • Stay in homestays – Support locals, cheaper than hotels
  • Eat local & seasonal – Street food & dhabas are affordable & low-waste
  • Choose offbeat places – Less crowded = less impact & lower prices
  • Pack reusable items – Bottle, bag, cutlery – reduce plastic
