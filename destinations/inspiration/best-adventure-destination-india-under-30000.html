Best Adventure Destinations in India Under ₹30,000

Adventure doesn't have to be expensive. These destinations offer thrilling experiences at budget prices.

Top Adventure Spots Under ₹30,000

  • Rishikesh, Uttarakhand – White-water rafting, bungee jumping
    ~₹20,000 – ₹28,000 (4–6 days)
  • Manali & Solang Valley – Paragliding, zorbing, trekking
    ~₹22,000 – ₹30,000 (5–7 days)
  • Gokarna, Karnataka – Surfing, beach treks
    ~₹18,000 – ₹28,000 (5–7 days)
  • Lonavala & Khandala – Valley rappelling, zip-line
    ~₹15,000 – ₹25,000 (3–5 days)
  • Dharamshala & McLeod Ganj – Trekking, paragliding
    ~₹20,000 – ₹29,000 (5–7 days)

Who These Adventures Are Best For

  • Young thrill-seekers & friends
  • Couples wanting active romance
  • Budget adventurers
  • First-time adrenaline junkies
