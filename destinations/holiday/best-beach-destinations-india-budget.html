Best Beach Destinations in India on a Budget (2026 Guide)

India has some of the world's most beautiful beaches – and many are surprisingly affordable. From lively Goa shores to quiet Gokarna sands, here's where your budget can take you.

Top Budget Beach Destinations

  • Goa – Lively beaches, nightlife, seafood
    ~₹20,000 – ₹45,000 (5–7 days)
  • Pondicherry – French vibe, calm shores
    ~₹18,000 – ₹40,000 (4–6 days)
  • Gokarna, Karnataka – Pristine, spiritual beaches
    ~₹22,000 – ₹42,000 (5–7 days)
  • Varkala, Kerala – Cliff beaches, yoga & ayurveda
    ~₹25,000 – ₹48,000 (6–8 days)
  • Digha, West Bengal – Relaxed, family-friendly
    ~₹20,000 – ₹38,000 (4–6 days)

Who These Beaches Are Best For

  • Couples wanting romance & relaxation
  • Families seeking safe, shallow shores
  • Budget travelers wanting sun & sea
  • Anyone craving a coastal escape
