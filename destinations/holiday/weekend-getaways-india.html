Best Weekend Getaways in India (2026)

Need a quick escape from the city? These weekend getaways are close, refreshing, and fit almost any budget.

Top Weekend Getaways

  • Lonavala & Khandala, Maharashtra – Hills, waterfalls, chikki
    ~₹12,000 – ₹25,000 (2–3 days)
  • Mount Abu, Rajasthan – Lakes, temples, cool weather
    ~₹15,000 – ₹28,000 (2–3 days)
  • Jim Corbett, Uttarakhand – Wildlife safari, forests
    ~₹18,000 – ₹30,000 (3 days)
  • Matheran, Maharashtra – No cars, toy train, hills
    ~₹10,000 – ₹22,000 (2 days)
  • Neemrana Fort Palace, Rajasthan – Heritage stay, zip-line
    ~₹20,000 – ₹35,000 (2–3 days)

Who These Getaways Are Best For

  • Busy professionals needing quick recharge
  • Couples on a romantic weekend
  • Families wanting short, safe trips
  • Friends looking for fun & adventure
