Best Hill Stations in India 2026 – Cool Escapes & Budget Ideas

Beat the summer heat with a refreshing trip to India's beautiful hill stations. Whether you want adventure, family time, or peaceful views, these destinations fit every budget.

Top Hill Stations You Can Visit in 2026

  • Manali, Himachal Pradesh – Snow views, adventure, riverside cafes
    ~₹25,000 – ₹45,000 (5–7 days)
  • Darjeeling, West Bengal – Tea gardens, toy train, Kanchenjunga views
    ~₹30,000 – ₹50,000 (6–8 days)
  • Shimla, Himachal Pradesh – Mall Road, colonial charm, toy train
    ~₹22,000 – ₹40,000 (4–6 days)
  • Ooty, Tamil Nadu – Lakes, botanical gardens, Nilgiri hills
    ~₹28,000 – ₹48,000 (5–7 days)
  • Mussoorie, Uttarakhand – Queen of Hills, waterfalls, cable car
    ~₹25,000 – ₹42,000 (4–6 days)

Who These Hill Stations Are Best For

  • Families seeking cool weather & scenic views
  • Couples wanting romantic getaways
  • Adventure lovers (trekking, paragliding)
  • Anyone escaping city heat in summer
