Ultimate Packing List for India Travel (2026)

Packing right saves money, space, and stress. India has diverse climates — here's the smart, season-wise checklist.

General Essentials (All Seasons)

  • Valid ID (Aadhaar/Passport) + photocopies
  • Power bank (India has frequent outages)
  • Reusable water bottle (stay hydrated)
  • Basic medicines + first-aid kit
  • Light backpack/daypack
  • Universal adapter + multi-plug
  • Quick-dry towel

Season-Wise Packing

  • Summer (March–June): Light cotton clothes, sunscreen (SPF 50+), hat, sunglasses, flip-flops, moisturizer
  • Monsoon (June–Sept): Waterproof jacket, quick-dry clothes, umbrella/poncho, anti-fungal powder, mosquito repellent
  • Winter (Nov–Feb): Warm layers, jacket, shawl, gloves, thermal wear (especially for hills)
  • Hill Stations: Fleece, windcheater, good walking shoes, socks
  • Beaches/Coastal: Swimwear, rash guard, flip-flops, beach towel

Budget & Space-Saving Tips

  • Pack multi-use items (scarf = towel + blanket + cover-up)
  • Choose light, quick-dry fabrics
  • Use compression bags
  • Buy toiletries locally (saves weight)
  • Leave expensive jewelry at home
