Complete Travel Cost Breakdown in India (2026)

Understanding every cost helps you plan better and avoid surprises. Here's the full breakdown of what a typical trip in India really adds up to.

Main Expense Categories

Category Typical Cost Range (per person) Notes
Intercity Travel₹5,000 – ₹25,000Train/bus (cheapest) vs flight (faster)
Local Transport₹2,000 – ₹8,000Cabs, autos, buses, rickshaws
Accommodation₹5,000 – ₹40,000Budget (₹1k/night) to 4-star (₹5k+)
Food & Meals₹4,000 – ₹12,000Local eateries vs restaurants
Sightseeing & Activities₹2,000 – ₹15,000Entry fees, guides, adventures
Buffer / Extras10–20% of totalShopping, emergencies, price changes

Hidden Costs to Watch Out For

  • Local transport between attractions
  • Entry fees & camera charges at monuments
  • Tips & small fees
  • Seasonal price spikes (peak season)
  • Extra meals/snacks
