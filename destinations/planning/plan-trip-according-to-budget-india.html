How to Plan a Trip According to Your Budget in India

The smartest way to travel: let your budget guide your destination — not the other way around. Here's the proven method used by thousands.

Step-by-Step Method

  1. Define your total realistic budget — Include travel, stay, food, activities, shopping, buffer (add 10–20% extra)
  2. Decide duration & group size — Longer trips = more cost per day; more people = shared accommodation savings
  3. Choose preferences — Beach, hills, culture, adventure, family, solo, foodie
  4. Get matching destinations — YatraMaker filters only what fits your budget 100%
  5. Review full cost breakdown — See itemized costs (travel, stay, local, buffer)
  6. Customize & save — Adjust dates, upgrade stay, add activities

Real Example: ₹45,000 Budget (5–6 days, 2 adults)

  • Possible: Goa, Kerala backwaters, Rajasthan short circuit
  • Cost split: Travel ₹15k, Stay ₹18k, Food ₹7k, Activities ₹5k
  • Style: Comfortable with buffer
