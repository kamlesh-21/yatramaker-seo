7-Day Himachal Itinerary Under ₹40,000 (2026 Realistic Plan)

Himachal offers hills, valleys, adventure & peace — all possible under ₹40,000 with smart planning.

Quick Overview

  • Duration: 7 days / 6 nights
  • Route: Shimla → Kullu/Manali → Dharamshala/Kasol → back
  • Travel: Train + local bus/taxi
  • Stay: Budget to mid-range hotels
  • Total cost: ~₹35,000 – ₹42,000 per person

Day-by-Day Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Shimla – Train to Kalka → toy train to Shimla, Mall Road, Jakhoo Temple
  2. Day 2: Shimla Exploration – Kufri, Christ Church, local market
  3. Day 3: Shimla → Manali – Bus/train to Kullu → Manali, Hadimba Temple
  4. Day 4: Manali – Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass (if open)
  5. Day 5: Manali → Kasol – Parvati Valley, cafes, river
  6. Day 6: Kasol → Dharamshala – McLeod Ganj, Dalai Lama Temple
  7. Day 7: Return – Dharamshala → train back home
