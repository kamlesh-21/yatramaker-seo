How to Travel India on a Fixed Budget (Smart Tips 2026)

Traveling India on a fixed budget is not only possible — it's enjoyable when done right. Here's how to make every rupee count.

Top Budget-Saving Strategies

  • Travel off-season — 20–40% cheaper hotels & flights
  • Use trains & buses — Sleeper class or state buses are cheapest
  • Stay in homestays/hostels — Authentic & ₹800–2,000/night
  • Eat local — Street food & dhabas = ₹200–500/day
  • Plan short trips — Weekend getaways or 4–5 days save big
  • Use YatraMaker — Matches destinations to your exact fixed budget

Realistic Daily Budget Examples

  • Tight budget: ₹1,500–2,500/day (sleeper train, homestay, local food)
  • Comfort budget: ₹3,000–5,000/day (3A train, mid-range hotel, restaurants)
  • Premium comfort: ₹7,000–12,000/day (flight, 4-star, activities)
