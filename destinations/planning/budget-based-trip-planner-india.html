Step-by-Step: How to Plan a Trip Based on Your Budget

  1. Know your total budget — Include everything: travel, stay, food, activities, shopping
  2. Decide duration & group size — 4–10 days, 1–6 people
  3. Choose preferences — Beach, hills, culture, adventure, family, solo
  4. Get realistic recommendations — YatraMaker shows only destinations that fit 100%
  5. See full cost breakdown — Travel, accommodation, local expenses, buffer
  6. Customize & save — Adjust dates, upgrade stay, add activities
