How YatraMaker Works

Unlike traditional travel websites that start with destinations, YatraMaker starts with your budget. You tell us how much you want to spend, and we help you discover destinations, itineraries, and holidays that fit comfortably within that budget.

This approach reduces uncertainty, overspending, and decision fatigue. It is ideal for families, couples, solo travellers, and anyone planning travel in India with a fixed or flexible budget.

Travel Styles and Budget Ranges

Whether you are planning a family holiday, couple trip, solo travel escape, group tour, weekend getaway, pilgrimage, beach break, hill station stay, wildlife retreat, or adventure travel, the budget-first approach helps you choose destinations that fit real costs.

  • Budget travel in India: INR 10,000 - INR 25,000
  • Mid-range trips: INR 25,000 - INR 50,000
  • Comfort trips: INR 50,000 - INR 1,00,000
  • Multi-city tours and longer holidays: INR 1,00,000+
